Our Mission

No one should go to sleep hungry — human or animal.

MealForAll is an independent feeding initiative started by a student with a simple belief — every life deserves a meal. We make your donation personal, visible, and real.

🐕

Feed Street Dogs

Nutritious rice and egg meals prepared fresh and delivered to street dogs in your city — filmed and sent to you personally.

🐱

Feed Street Cats

Fresh rice and fish meals prepared for street cats — filmed and delivered to you personally on WhatsApp.

🍽️

Feed Humans

Hot khichdi meals distributed to people in need — with your name on the packet as a mark of your kindness.

📲

100% Transparent

Every rupee is documented. Every meal is filmed. You see exactly where your donation goes — no exceptions.

How It Works

Simple. Personal. Real.

From your donation to their meal in 4 easy steps — with proof sent directly to you.

1

You Donate

Choose to feed a dog or a human. Share your name, photo, and occasion with us.

2

We Print Your Label

Your name and photo goes on the meal packet — making it truly yours.

3

We Feed & Film

We go out, feed, and record the entire moment — mentioning your name on camera.

4

You Receive Video

A personal WhatsApp video of your meal being served — your memory to keep forever.
